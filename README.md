
# Becoming Itumeleng 💜

An AI agent that knows who I want to be and helps me get there.

She curates my style, keeps my personal brand consistent, tracks my goals, and gives me honest accountability nudges when I need them. Think of her as the most organised, most stylish, most supportive version of me… in code.

Powered by the [Strands Agents SDK](https://strandsagents.com/) and running on Amazon Bedrock with Claude Sonnet 4.

## Why I Built This

I believe that the best way to learn something is to build something personal with it. So instead of building another chatbot or another generic demo, I asked myself: *what if I could code the woman I'm becoming?*

That question turned into this agent. She knows my goals, my wardrobe, my brand guidelines, and my values. She doesn't judge. She doesn't shame. She just shows up, checks in, and reminds me of who I said I wanted to be.

## What She Does

**Style Curator** — Suggests outfits based on the occasion, weather, my mood, and what's actually in my wardrobe. No more standing in front of the closet for 20 minutes.

**Brand Guide** — Keeps my personal brand consistent across YouTube, LinkedIn, and Medium. Tone, messaging, visual identity… she knows the playbook.

**Accountability Partner** — Tracks my goals and checks in on progress. Kind but honest. She won't let me slide.

**Consistency Coach** — Helps me build daily habits and tracks my streaks. Because consistency beats intensity, every time.

**Hype Woman** — Celebrates my wins (big and small), boosts my confidence before events or recordings, and reminds me how far I've come on the days when I forget.
